Summer Camp

Come join us this summer for a fabulous time with our fabulous horses! Boys and girls ages 5-14 welcome. Children will learn the fundamentals of riding, horse care, and the value of horses in our lives. These horse camps will provide experience for kids to gain confidence around horses, independence, and leadership skills.

Dates

  • Session 1: June 16th, 17th, and 18th

  • Session 2: July 7th, 8th, and 9th

  • Session 3 (advanced): July 28th, 29th and 30th

Pricing

  • $75/child non-refundable deposit

  • $80/day

  • $200 if attends all 3 days

Staff

Your kids will have fun and be safe with our enthusiastic camp team. With years of horse experience under their belt, our team will do their best to make sure your kids have the best experience possible.

Schedule

  • Daily riding
  • Horsemanship
  • Horse care
  • Games
  • Lunch and snacks
  • Arts and crafts
  • Teamwork
  • Barn chores
  • Lots of fun!!
